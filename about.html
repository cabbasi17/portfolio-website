About Me

My Story

Hi, I’m Cienna! I’m a UI designer with a unique background in early childhood special education. Over the years, I developed a deep sense of empathy, patience, and creativity while working with children with diverse abilities. These experiences have not only shaped my approach to design but have also given me valuable skills in organization and problem-solving. I learned how to adapt to individual needs, create personalized strategies, and ensure that every child had the tools they needed to succeed. These lessons have become the foundation of my design philosophy, as I approach each project with a focus on accessibility, inclusivity, and usercentric solutions. Transitioning into UI design felt like a natural evolution for me. The same principles I applied in education—understanding different learning styles, simplifying complex concepts, and building supportive environments—are all vital to creating intuitive, user-friendly digital experiences. I’m passionate about designing interfaces that cater to people of all abilities, ensuring that every user, whether they have a disability or not, can navigate with ease. My goal is always to eliminate barriers, making technology more accessible and enjoyable for everyone. My experience has taught me that great design doesn’t just look good—it works for the people who use it. I approach each project with a focus on user experience and inclusivity, striving to create designs that not only meet functional requirements but also enhance the overall user journey. Whether it’s optimizing a website for screen readers, ensuring color contrast for users with visual impairments, or simplifying navigation, I’m driven by the desire to create seamless, engaging experiences that empower users. Combining my background in education with my design skills allows me to build platforms that make a meaningful impact in the lives of those who use them. Outside of work, I’m an avid traveler and adventurer. Growing up in different countries has given me a unique perspective on the world and a deep appreciation for diverse cultures and experiences. I’m always inspired by new places, people, and ideas, and I bring that curiosity and creativity into my work. Whether I’m hiking through nature or exploring new cities, I find inspiration in every journey.
